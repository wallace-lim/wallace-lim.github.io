Candidate Tracker Slackbot

Upsilon Pi Epsion, a CS honor society, holds semesterly initiation for the top 1/3 CS students, having candidates to professional requirements to help them gain industry experience. Prior to slackbot, all requirements were tracked on a Google spreadsheet making it inconvenient for not only candidates to look up their remaining requirements, but also for officers to check off 100+ potential candidate. Slackbot tackles this issue by centralizing all look up and check off to a 4-5 Slack commands in our main way of communication, Slack.
